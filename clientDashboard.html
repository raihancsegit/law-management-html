Financial Documents

Required
Bank statements from all accounts (checking, savings, business) for the past 6 months. Pay stubs showing current income, tax returns for the past 2 years including all schedules and attachments. Profit and loss statements if self-employed. Records of any other income sources including rental income, social security, disability, unemployment benefits, or side business income. Investment account statements, retirement account statements, and any documentation of financial assets or accounts that have been closed in the past year.

Property Documents

Optional
Property deeds for all real estate owned, mortgage statements and loan documents, property tax assessments and payment records. Vehicle titles, registration documents, and loan information for all cars, trucks, motorcycles, boats, or recreational vehicles. Appraisals or market valuations for real estate and vehicles. Documentation of any property transfers, sales, or gifts made in the past 2 years. Rental property income and expense records if applicable.

Debt Documentation

Required
Credit card statements for all accounts for the past 6 months, including closed accounts. Personal loan documents, student loan statements, medical debt records, and collection notices. Correspondence from creditors, debt settlement agreements, and any payment plans currently in place. Documentation of secured debts like mortgages and car loans. Records of any debts that have been paid off, forgiven, or discharged in the past year. Proof of any disputes with creditors or credit reporting agencies.

Uploaded files:

credit_card_stmt.pdf

Income Verification

Required
Employment verification letters from current and previous employers within the past 2 years. Social Security statements, disability award letters, unemployment benefit records, and workers' compensation documentation. Self-employment income records including 1099s, business receipts, and expense documentation. Pension, retirement, or annuity income statements. Documentation of rental income, alimony, child support, or any other regular income sources. Records of seasonal or irregular income, side business earnings, and any cash income received.

Legal Documents

Optional
Divorce decrees, separation agreements, and prenuptial or postnuptial agreements. Child custody orders, child support agreements, and alimony documentation. Court judgments, liens, or garnishment orders. Lawsuit documents, settlement agreements, and legal correspondence. Power of attorney documents, wills, and trust agreements. Any pending litigation or legal proceedings documentation. Immigration documents if applicable, and any other legal contracts or agreements that may affect your financial situation.

Other Documents

Optional
Insurance policies including life, health, auto, and property insurance documentation. Business documents if you own a business, including operating agreements, partnership documents, and business financial records. Personal property valuations for jewelry, art, collectibles, or other valuable items. Documentation of gifts given or received over $600 in the past year. Records of gambling winnings or losses. Any other financial documents, contracts, or agreements that may be relevant to your bankruptcy case and financial situation.
