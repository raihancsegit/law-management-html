General Questions

Bankruptcy is a legal process that helps individuals and businesses eliminate or repay their debts under court protection. It provides relief from overwhelming debt and a fresh financial start.

Application Process

You'll need recent pay stubs, tax returns, bank statements, a list of debts and creditors, and asset information. We'll provide a complete checklist during your consultation.

From initial consultation to filing typically takes 2-4 weeks, depending on how quickly you can gather required documents. The entire bankruptcy process takes 3-6 months.

You'll attend one brief meeting with the bankruptcy trustee (Meeting of Creditors). We'll prepare you thoroughly and be there with you. It typically lasts 5-10 minutes.

Legal Information

Chapter 7 eliminates most debts in 3-6 months, while Chapter 13 creates a 3-5 year payment plan. We'll help determine which chapter is best for your situation.

Bankruptcy will initially lower your credit score, but many clients see improvement within 1-2 years. It's often better than continuing to miss payments and accumulate debt.

While legally possible, bankruptcy law is complex and mistakes can be costly. Having an experienced attorney significantly increases your chances of a successful outcome.
